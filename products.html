Grimson Mk- I

Reinforced tanto engineered for strength and control

Intended Applications: Construction and Field Use

The MK-I is GRIMSON’s flagship launch model, built around the strength and control of a reinforced tanto blade geometry. Designed for demanding utility work, the blade’s angular tip provides exceptional penetration strength and durability where conventional profiles tend to fail.

Clean lines, durable steel, and a controlled working profile make the MK-I a dependable field tool rather than a decorative piece. Its balanced proportions and reinforced construction are engineered for real tasks, where reliability and precision matter more than excess.
